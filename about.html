Comprehensive San Francisco Solar Panel Developers

Being Diamond Certified means that we respect you, your budget, and your energy needs. We have a legacy of happy residential customers throughout California, having performed thousands of jobs. Let IT Integrated Services, Inc. and our network be your turnkey solar solution for years to come.

1. We Offer a True System Production Guarantee —We do so much more than just cover whether or not the various components of your system work at all; we cover overall production. When your system doesn’t live up to its true potential, we’ll come out and make things right. And this coverage isn’t vague or in unspecific terms either—we contractually guarantee your system will harvest a minimum threshold of its potential energy or we will (A) fix it at no expense to you and (B) compensate you for any gaps in production.

2. We Protect Your Roof as Well as Your Solar System –We understand that your solar system becomes an integral part of your roof, so we protect the roof the system is mounted to with a 25-year workmanship (roof attachments) warranty. If your roof ever experiences a problem because of our solar mounting solution, we’ll get it fixed at no cost to you. We only use premium labor and roof mounting materials for optimal, long-lasting results.

3. We Use Perfectly-Matched Platinum Technology— We believe that you should never have to sacrifice energy because some aspect of your solar energy system can’t handle the power you produce. Our robust inverter-optimizer configuration ensures that we take advantage of the maximum amount of power your solar system harvests and converts to clean power. Our smart design also reduces power losses that are suffered through excess wiring. This translates into a more efficient system that saves you more money, accelerates your rate of return and payback period and further reduces your carbon footprint.
