HOME BACKUP POWER. COMFORT. SECURITY. CONVENIENCE. - Generac

A permanently installed Generac home backup generator protects your home automatically. It runs on natural gas or liquid propane (LP) fuel and sits outside just like a central air conditioning unit. A home backup generator delivers power directly to your home’s electrical system, backing up your entire home or just the most essential items.

Severe weather is the single leading cause of power outages in the United States. The amount and severity of these outages has spiked dramatically over the past decade, causing physical, emotional and economic hardship for many Americans.

Automatic Operation

Disasters can happen at any moment, on any day, while you are home or away. It is not a question of IF, it is a matter of WHEN you will need to have power during an EMERGENCY. To most having that SECURITY and the COMFORT of knowing that your HOME will still have POWER during one of those moments are beyond measure. Installing an auto-backup generator to support your existing power configuration is a good way to provide that added protection and extra power to wait out a storm. ​

These systems start when the power is cut off, so you don't have to worry about having to be there to start and turn off your home backup generator because it does that automatically, even when you're away from home or even distracted inside your home at the time of the incident. Every moment counts when an emergency is occurring, so you can focus on what is most important, your family.
