Leading the Way for Electric Vehicles in California

EV charging stations offer a number of benefits overcharging your car with a standard 120-volt outlet. Not only will your green car use a green source of energy but charging your vehicle won’t be tied to your city’s electrical grid, saving you money over the long run.

Benefits of installing an EV charging station include:

  • You lock in a lower energy cost for the future and save money
  • You free yourself from supporting “dirty” fossil fuels
  • EV charging stations take up minimal space while offering maximum mileage
  • Stations charge your car faster than traditional outlets
  • Your station pays for itself in as little as two years, depending on your car
